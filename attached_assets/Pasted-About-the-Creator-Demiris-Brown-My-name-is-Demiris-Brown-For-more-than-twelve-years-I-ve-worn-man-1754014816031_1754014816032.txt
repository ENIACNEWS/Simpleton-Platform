About the Creator: Demiris Brown

My name is Demiris Brown. For more than twelve years I’ve worn many hats in the jewelry world-jeweler, diamond trader, certified grader and gemstone appraiser, and pawnshop general manager. Over that time I’ve seen the same questions and frustrations come up again and again: “What is this gold really worth?”, “How do I know a diamond’s true market value?”, “Where can I quickly compare prices without wading through pages of technical jargon?” Simpletonapp was born to answer those questions, strip away the confusion, and level the playing field for anyone-whether you’re a seasoned pro, a small-town jeweler, or someone who simply inherited a piece of family jewelry and wants to know what it’s worth.

Why I Built Simpletonapp  
I’ve spent my career inside cases and vaults, grading thousands of stones and running daily pricing drills. I know how fast markets move-gold and silver spot prices shift by the minute, diamond price guides are updated every quarter, and Rolex values can spike overnight. Yet when I step back, I realize that most online tools either assume you’re an expert or leave out critical details. Simpletonapp bridges that gap. Our gold calculator offers live spot pricing, a “custom-price” input for veterans who want full control, and a batch-total feature for mixed-purity lots. Our diamond calculator factors in size, color, clarity, and cut. We even built live tickers for gold, silver, diamonds/and yes, stocks-so you can see the market pulse in real time.

My Mission  
I want to empower the next generation of jewelry professionals and curious collectors. If you’re just starting out, Simpletonapp gives you the same accurate pricing data I’ve relied on behind the counter. If you’ve been in the industry for decades, you’ll appreciate our custom-price overrides and batch tools. Our educational sections walk you through diamond grading basics, Rolex model histories, and best practices for buying and selling. This platform is my “baby”-it started as a simple calculator on my desk and has grown into a full-featured resource for the world.

Thank you for stopping by. I hope Simpletonapp makes your buying, selling, and learning experiences smoother, more transparent, and-above all—simple. If you ever have questions, feedback, or just want to share a success story, I’d love to hear from you.
